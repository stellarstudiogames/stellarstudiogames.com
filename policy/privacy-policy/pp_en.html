Stellar Studio Games does not collect personal information that can directly identify a user, such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

Our applications may offer in-app purchases (IAP). All IAP transactions are handled through app distribution platforms (Google Play Store, Apple App Store), and we do not collect or store any payment information. Any information provided to complete a transaction is managed by the respective platform in accordance with its privacy policies.

4. Data We Do Not Collect

Stellar Studio Games does not access or collect information related to contacts, calendar, text messages, photos, or any other data stored on the user's device.
